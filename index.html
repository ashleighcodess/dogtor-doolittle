Who We Are

Meet Austin, the Dogtor

Dogtor Doolittle was founded by Austin Mendoza out of New Jersey. In 2020, Austin rescued a puppy that was poorly behaved. He was forced to teach him how to settle down, and a love for training began.

Later that year, Austin was told that he was the modern day Doctor Doolittle, for his passion, love for animals, and strong belief that all dogs of any size and breed can be rehabilitated, and thus Dogtor Doolittle was born!

"Not an animal doctor but I speak dog"
